  • You will most likely be able to do your first demo lesson within a week of turning in your application, so the process is really quick.
  • The interview will be done online through the VIPKID portal.
  • In the interview: be able to show your ability to explain the activities.
  • Be prepared

You will then need to watch the training videos that VIPKID provides.

  • These videos will tell you the standard of teaching, how to use tools in the classroom, and about the cirriculum taught.
  • VIPKID uses all US Common Core Standard curriculum.

Next, you will be asked to teach a full 25 minutes mock lesson. Then, once all the needed documents have been sent in, you become a ESL Teacher for VIPKID!
